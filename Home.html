Our priority is for your child to feel cared for, loved, heard and seen. Your little one has an identity and our job is to free it to flourish. It is not our goal to force ideals upon your child. With your assistance and teamwork, your child will thrive and grow in an environment that encourages and supports your child.

Parents, Caregivers and Family, we are here for you!

What you can expect and what your children will learn.

  1. We practice the principles of gentle and conscious parenting and coaching.
  2. Our program is customized to the needs of each individual child.
  3. We teach the children about sharing, being kind to self and others, using your voice for expression and we build their confidence in their decisions/actions/behaviors.
  • Expression of self
  • Kind treatment of self and others

We believe in your child's ability to learn, grow and flourish in this environment.
